As you may have seen, there has been a redesign of this site to make it seem more professionall and at the same time stabilise the backend whilst also focusing on the UI and UX elements of the site.

Changes in the menus and how they are made have been made with the help of [ootstrap](https://getbootstrap.com/) and Liquid that is used in the backend, as to make it a better experience for the user wether they are from a phone, table or a desktop.

This redesign has also shown me that static sites a lot limited than dynamic site, in terms of user interaction the site was very limited and that has come to an end. With the help of [Disqus](https://disqus.com/), I have introduced a comment section that can be found in the bottom of every post, thus now there can be a space where the readers are able to interact under every post. 
I would like to note that the disqus comments have been setup in a way where you dont need to register, just checking the box "I'd rather post as a guest" will provide you with a form that only needs an email and a Name.

Whilst these changes are minimal, I hope that they can better the site exoerience for new users and old users alike.
